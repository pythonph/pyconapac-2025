What is PyCon APAC?

PyCon APAC is the regional counterpart of PyCon in the Asia-Pacific region

It brings together Python developers, enthusiasts, and industry leaders from across APAC for a unique conference experience. Hosted annually in one of the participating countries, PyCon APAC serves as a key platform for fostering collaboration, learning, and innovation.

Past host countries include Singapore, Japan, Taiwan, South Korea, Malaysia, Philippines, Thailand, and Indonesia. This year, we are excited to host PyCon APAC 2025 in the Philippines, following the success of PyCon PH 2024.

Building on the foundation of PyCon PH, the largest gathering of Python Developers in the Philippines. PyCon APAC 2025 expands our reach to an international audience. It creates opportunities for deeper collaboration and knowledge sharing across diverse cultures and industries throughout the Asia-Pacific region.
