The Python Conference (PyCon) is a volunteer-run, not-for-profit conference centered around the Python Programming Language.

It originated in the United States but is also held in many other countries with active Python developer communities.

In the Philippines, we've been organizing PyCon Philippines (PyCon PH) annually since 2012.

We bring in both local and international speakers from diverse backgrounds, making PyCon PH a globally enriching experience.
